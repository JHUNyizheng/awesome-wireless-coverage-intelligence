# Artifact Index

This page provides human-readable views of the machine-readable [artifact ledger](artifact_availability_ledger.csv). Canonical status, license, version, and verification notes remain in the CSV.

## Browse by Linked Work or Resource

- RadioMapSeer and RadioUNet: dataset and reconstruction notebooks.
- DeepMIMO: configurable channel-data toolchain.
- DeepREM: dataset, trained-model record, and demo record.
- SpectrumNet: multiband and 3D dataset line.
- UrbanRadio3D and RadioDiff: volumetric dataset, recommended split, training code, and request-based weights.
- RadioMapMotion: dynamic dataset, code, and forecast protocol.
- 3DiRM3200 and R2Net: indoor 3D dataset, notebooks, and partial checkpoints.
- CKMImageNet: gain, delay, AOA, and AOD data.
- Grid-Free Radio Map Estimation: data and executable estimation code.
- RadioLAM, REM-Net+, and measured urban 3D GPR: result cards and protocol disclosures.
- Resource-first entries: RMDirectionalBerlin, MobiSim, Multi-config Radiomap Dataset, and the Real-world CKM Dataset.
