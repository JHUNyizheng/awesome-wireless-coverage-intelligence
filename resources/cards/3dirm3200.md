## Protocol Checks

- Indoor versus outdoor subset.
- Receiver-height split.
- Training-set size.
- R2Net variant and checkpoint availability.
- Whether inference speed or model size is reported alongside error.

## WCI Interpretation

3DiRM3200 helps isolate height as a modeling variable. For WCI, it is most useful
when interpreted as height-aware reconstruction evidence rather than a complete
low-altitude network benchmark.
